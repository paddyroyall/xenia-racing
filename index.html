Xenia is an X362 Sport (36 foot cruiser-racer). We race IRC based in Helford, mainly around the south coast of the UK. We do regattas such as Dart Week, Cork Week and Falmouth week and also coastal races around the Cornish coast (eg Falmouth to Fowey) and cross channel.

The idea is to have fun, we are often on the podium in Cornish racing and we like to be in the top third of the fleet at higher-profile regattas.

We are lucky to sail and race in some beautiful waters. Xenia lives on the Helford River with two spectacular waterside pubs, the Ferryboat Inn on the north side of the mooring and the Shipwrights' Arms on the south side.

Most of our local racing is run from Falmouth. We are an out-of-port boat (most of us live outside Cornwall), we don't do that many events. We have a wonderfully commited core crew who have raced for a number of years but we are always looking for experienced crew who would like to do 4-8 events of high-quality racing per season.
